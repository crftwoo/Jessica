# Design System: Stadler Logística Elite

## 1. Color Palette (Refined Brand Identity)
- **Primary (Deep Navy):** `#0F172A` - Base for dark modes and headers.
- **Accent (Vibrant Red):** `#EF4444` - Used sparingly for high-impact CTAs and details.
- **Secondary (Steel Blue):** `#1E293B` - For cards and backgrounds.
- **Surface (Ghost White):** `#F8FAFC` - Main background for light versions.
- **Success/Safe:** `#10B981` - Used for status indicators.

## 2. Typography
- **Headings:** `Outfit` (Sans-serif, modern, geometric). Bold weights for authority.
- **Body:** `Inter` (Sans-serif, high legibility).
- **Hierarchy:** Large H1s (64px+) with tight tracking for a premium feel.

## 3. Spatial Design
- **Gutter:** 32px (8 units).
- **Padding:** Generous white space (96px+ between sections).
- **Grids:** 12-column layout with offset elements for visual interest.

## 4. Components & Interactions
- **Glassmorphism:** Subtle blur effects on navigation and modals.
- **Buttons:** Smooth transitions, scaling on hover, and custom drop shadows.
- **Cards:** Border-gradient effects and soft shadows.

## 5. Visual Language
- **Imagery:** High-quality photos of the Stadler fleet (white trucks) against clean backgrounds.
- **Shapes:** Clean lines, rounded corners (8px radius), and professional grids.
